About Us

About
Enjad

Enjad Student Organization is a non-profit, non-governmental group headquartered at the Faculty of Engineering, Helwan University. The organization focuses on community development by working on mechatronics and chemical engineering projects across industrial, environmental, and medical fields. Enjad executes and enhances multiple projects annually, aiming to deliver cost-effective solutions compared to commercial alternatives.

Our
Mission

Our exhibition emphasizes innovation and solutions to community challenges, showcasing our dedication through engaging displays and presentations. We aim to inspire and educate, sharing insights on advanced control, robotics, and sustainability. Beyond achievements, we focus on fostering connections, collaborations, and inspiring success in others.

Our
Objective

Enjad's primary goal is to serve the community by developing valuable, cost-effective projects that benefit specific societal segments and transform into marketable products. Our innovative projects introduce unique features unavailable in the market. Beyond personal and professional growth, we focus on creating a positive impact through our services, outreach events, and community-driven initiatives.
